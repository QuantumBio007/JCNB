### Workflow definition

***Task definition***

Build a Python prototype that generates first-pass stakeholder outreach emails for JCNB Biotech Consulting from structured notes, then evaluate output quality across a small stable set of outreach scenarios

**Workflow chosen**  
Drafting first-pass stakeholder outreach emails for JCNB Biotech Consulting.

**User**  
The primary user is a JCNB founder or consultant preparing outreach to ministries of health, major hospitals, and NGOs in Latin America.

**Input**  
Structured notes about the target stakeholder, including: organization type and role, country, primary oncology drug supply problem, relevant JCNB service to highlight, desired tone, and call to action.

**Output**  
A concise outreach email draft with a subject line and body text tailored to that stakeholder, plus a simple flag indicating whether the draft needs human review before sending.

**Why this task is valuable to automate**  
Outreach emails are repetitive but high-effort to write well, and quality matters for busy senior health decision-makers. A drafting assistant can save time, enforce consistent positioning of JCNB’s services, and free the human user to focus on judgment and relationship-building rather than wordsmithing from scratch.
